About Khumtech

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Khumtech started as a small tech repair shop with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional technology services to our community. What began as a passion for fixing broken devices has grown into a comprehensive technology solutions provider.

Over the years, we've expanded our services to include custom software development and device sales, always maintaining our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of satisfied customers, from individual consumers to large enterprises, helping them leverage technology to achieve their goals.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses with reliable technology solutions that enhance productivity, creativity, and connectivity. We strive to be the trusted partner for all your tech needs.

Our Vision

To become South Africa's leading technology service provider, known for innovation, excellence, and customer-centric solutions that drive digital transformation across all sectors.
